# Just Ralph It

> Rigorous intent. Autonomous execution.

Most vibe-coding tools start writing code after one or two prompt. Just Ralph It makes you think very, very rigorously first—so much that you might even discard the idea—and then lets an autonomous agent build the whole thing, unattended, with full power on a VPS (a.k.a. *god mode*).

---

## Agents

There are two agents:

- **Ralphy**: The interviewer. He maps your mind to beads issues until there are no gaps at all.
- **Ralph**: The builder. He solves just one issue and leaves notes for his future self if needed.

They're designed to be generic at first and specific as the project progresses. Their system prompts are written as concisely as possible, and that's by design too, meant to avoid context bloat. That way, we not only give them room to absorb more project context, but also make them perform better.
